About Echoear

Echoear is an advanced AI application designed to estimate Pulmonary Artery Pressure (PAP) and Ejection Fraction (EF) using stethoscope signals, providing heart health insights comparable to traditional echocardiography.

Key Features

  • Accurate PAP Estimation: Using cutting-edge algorithms to estimate pulmonary artery pressure.
  • EF Calculation: AI-driven approach to estimate the heart's ejection fraction for reliable heart health monitoring.
  • Non-Invasive Monitoring: Utilizes simple stethoscope signals, making heart monitoring accessible and convenient.

Technology Behind Echoear

Echoear combines AI and signal processing to interpret stethoscope audio, analyzing complex heart sounds to produce PAP and EF estimates in real-time. Built to be compatible with Bluetooth stethoscopes, it enables mobile, remote assessments without the need for ultrasound equipment.
